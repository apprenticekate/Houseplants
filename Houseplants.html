Houseplants

A guide to my current plant friends.

African Violets

  • Sun: Bright, indirect
  • Water: Let dry between waterings. (Every week or so)
  • Humidity: Add humidifier in winter
  • Pet-Safe: Yes

Cast Iron Plant

  • Sun: Bright, indirect
  • Water: Let dry between waterings. (Every two weeks or so)
  • Humidity: Likes a humidifier in winter
  • Pet-Safe: Yes

Jade

  • Sun: Bright direct
  • Water: Let dry between waterings. (Once a month or so)
  • Humidity: Can handle a dry house
  • Pet-Safe: No

Pothos

  • Sun: Indirect; can handle low light
  • Water: Let dry between waterings. (Every three weeks or so)
  • Humidity: Can handle a dry house
  • Pet-Safe: No
