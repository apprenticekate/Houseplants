Houseplants

A guide to my current plant friends.


> Resources <
African Violets
  • Sun: Indirect
  • Water: When top of soil is dry
  • Humidity: Add humidifier in winter
  • Pet-Safe: Yes
Cast Iron Plant
  • Sun: Indirect
  • Water: When top two inches of soil are dry
  • Humidity: Add humidifier in winter
  • Pet-Safe: Yes
Jade
  • Sun: Bright, direct
  • Water: When leaves begin to wrinkle
  • Humidity: Tolerates dry air
  • Pet-Safe: No
Panda Plant
  • Sun: Bright, direct
  • Water: Sparingly, but deeply
  • Humidity: Tolerates dry air
  • Pet-Safe: No
Pothos
  • Sun: Indirect
  • Water: When leaves begin to wilt
  • Humidity: Tolerates dry air
  • Pet-Safe: No
